Frequently Asked Questions

Will customers know they're talking to AI?

TitanFlow operates transparently and can be configured to identify itself as automated assistance when appropriate. The focus is on helpful, accurate responses that maintain trust.

What if the AI doesn't understand a customer?

TitanFlow is designed to escalate uncertain situations to your team rather than guess. Conservative operation ensures customer trust is maintained.

Can I customize the workflows for my business?

Yes. You define your customer journey stages, success conditions, and messaging. TitanFlow executes your workflows, not generic templates.

How quickly can I get started?

Most businesses are automating basic follow-ups within their first week. Complex workflows can be built gradually as you get comfortable with the system.
